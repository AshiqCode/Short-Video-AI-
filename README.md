### 🤖 Intelligent Video Transformation
Turn hours of footage into viral-ready content instantly. This AI-powered tool analyzes long-form videos and extracts the most engaging segments based on your custom duration and quantity settings.

### ✨ Core Features
- 🧠 **Smart Extraction:** AI identifies high-retention "hooks" from long videos.
- ⏱️ **Custom Duration:** Set precise timeframes (15s, 30s, 60s) for your shorts.
- 🔢 **Batch Generation:** Generate multiple shorts from a single source in one click.
- 🎨 **Adaptive Layout:** Automatically crops and optimizes for vertical mobile viewing.

---

### 🚀 Usage Guide

1. **Input:** Paste your video link or upload the source file.
2. **Parameters:** - Define **Shorts Duration** (e.g., 45 seconds).
   - Define **Shorts Count** (e.g., 5 clips).
3. **Generate:** The AI processes the logic and isolates the top engagement peaks.
4. **Export:** Download your ready-to-post vertical videos.
